PAYTABLE

243 ways to win. Wins = multiplier × ways × play amount. Left-to-right consecutive reels.

Wild substitutes for all regular symbols except scatter. Title Belt lands can trigger the Championship Multiplier: a random 1×–10× boost to the current spin win where supported by the round result.

3 or more scatter symbols trigger Free Spins Bonus. Pick a fighter: fewer spins award a stronger fighter multiplier (3×–6×). When the picked fighter lands, that reel becomes a Fighter Takeover reel and expands the winning ways.

VS Bonus: When the Title Belt lands between two fighters, pick your contender for a 2×–5× bet multiplier prize. 50/50 odds — trust your gut.

Final Bell Duel: Buy entry into a 5-round back-to-back title fight gauntlet for 100× your base play amount. Pick your fighter each round — winners pay 2×–5× multipliers with fighter takeovers. How many rounds can you survive?

Configured RTP: ~96.5%. Maximum win: 5000× play amount. Volatility: high. Feature mode cost: 100× base play amount where enabled by the Remote Game Server.

All balances, plays, wins, feature availability, and final settlement are controlled by the Remote Game Server. Browser animations are illustrative only.
